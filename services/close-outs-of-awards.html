Close Out of Awards

Assist local partners with administrative and financial actions and all required work with the award that must be completed prior to the award close out.

  • Close out letter to the donor.
  • List all inventory acquired using donor funds.
  • Assist with final financial submissions.
  • Accrual of employee salaries pay outs.
  • Assist with final audits.
  • Report VAT if relevant.
  • Send financial reports and final vouchers.
  • Program and final contract report.
