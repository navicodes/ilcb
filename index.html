IMPACTFUL LOCAL CAPACITY BUILDERS

   OBJECTIVES

ILCB main objectives revolve around the following:

  • To strengthen capacity of local Non Government organizations (NGO's) to implement high quality services.
  • Strengthen capacity of local governments and introduce best practice to manage donor funds.

   SERVICES

Audit preparedness

This includes assisting NGOs to prepare for audits, procuring audit firms, correcting audit findings and following donor audit rules and regulations

Audits for Vouchers

This includes building capacity of NGOs to respond to audit requests such as vouchers selected by auditors, reviewing vouchers for completeness and compliance with donor rules.

Local NGO/Partner Risk Assessmants

This includes evaluation of the existing controls, either as part of an official audit, assessment or in preparation for an audit, to see if the controls are in place that satisfy donor rules and identify weaknesses.

Fraud-Awareness training

We offer a continuing need to raise staff awareness of their responsibility to safeguard public resources against the risk of fraud.

Sub-Award Management

Risk Assessment for new partners
